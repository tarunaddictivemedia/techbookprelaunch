FAQs

What is a Techbook?+

Techbook is an intelligent book designed to transform traditional textbook-driven learning. The TECHBOOK brings three cutting-edge technologies and NCF aligned curriculum to address key learning challenges among school students today.

For which grades is The Techbook available?+

The Techbook is currently available for Grades 1 -8.

For which subjects is The Techbook available?+

The Techbook is currently available for Math, Science & English.

How can I get TECHBOOK for my school?+

Techbook is currently available by Invitation Only. To get your exclusive invite code, either get in touch with your Pinnacle Representative OR fill out your details here -<4AM to put><>

Can parents purchase a Techbook directly for their child?+

No. Techbook is available only for students in select new-age schools that pre-book. Check with your school to apply for Techbook.

How is TECHBOOK different from traditional textbooks?+

The TECHBOOK addresses the limitations of traditional textbooks by introducing a personalized and interactive learning experience. In classrooms with varying learning levels, the TECHBOOK enables personalised instruction for each student.

What is ARI? How does it improve the learning of students?+

Traditional 2D textbooks limit students' ability to grasp complex concepts that are inherently 3D in nature. The TECHBOOK brings subjects such as Science and Mathematics to life with ARI enabling students to interact with topics in 3D using any device like mobile phone/tablet. The teachers can also use this augmented reality technology in their classrooms by casting on their existing hardware.

What is IRA? How does it improve reading fluency of students?+

For language learning, TECHBOOK’s IRA acts as a personal tutor, reading books aloud to students, while also listening to a student’s reading and providing real-time feedback on their reading fluency and pronunciation.

What is PIE? How does it help in improving subject mastery?+

With PIE (Personalized Interactive Exercises), students get limitless exercises that are adaptive in nature. So students can master subjects at their own pace using any device like a mobile phone/tablet, making learning fun and continuous.

Which technologies are available in which subjects? +

TECHBOOK English is powered by IRA & PIE. TECHBOOK Science has ARI & PIE. TECHBOOK Math has ARI & PIE.
